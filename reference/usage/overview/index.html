    Overview

    The Chaos Toolkit implements a specific workflow that intends to help you discover, explore, build, run and , most importantly, learn from your chaos engineering experiments.

    • Install or Upgrade the Chaos Toolkit.
    • Discover what can be explored in your target environment.
    • Init to bootstrap your own, new chaos engineering experiments based on what has been discovered.
    • Run your chaos engineering experiments.
    • Produce great reports based on the findings of your chaos engineering experiments.
    • Notify your team to make your experiments visible to all.
